I am an Indian-born, Boston based portrait and travel photographer. My work focuses on blending art, fashion, and raw human emotions into images that tell a story. I love to travel and photograph different cultures and would ideally like to do that for a few months every year, but my lack of unlimited vacation time prevents me from doing that. Apart from portraits and travel, I photograph events where I focus on capturing candid human emotions and beautioful moments. If you like my style of photography and think that we'll be a good fit for what you have in mind, feel free to contact me using the contact form on this website. You can also order prints for any of the photos in the website using the print oredering link on my protfolio page.
